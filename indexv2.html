Project C45: Rubies and Emeralds

Create the Rubies and Emeralds Game

Mrs. Richie is a Billionaire who loves the X and O game and is a Grand Master in it. She regularly keeps her coveted title every year, defeating every player in it.

You got a chance to meet her in the World Gaming Expo. She wants you to design a new game for her. This one is called Emeralds and Rubies.

Mrs. Richie also showed you the game she has which is played with real emeralds and rubies.

In this project, you will have to practice and apply what you have learnt in the class and create the Rubies and Emeralds Game. This is a similar game, where the buttons when pressed, show a Ruby or an Emerald.
